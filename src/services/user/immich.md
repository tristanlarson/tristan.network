Immich is amazing, and it doesn't even take that much setup! It looks just like Google Photos so nobody's out of their depth, and there's even this beautiful tool called [google-photos-exif](https://github.com/mattwilson1024/google-photos-exif) that'll combine your google-takeout photos with their respective metadata json files. It was surprisingly easy to get set up.
