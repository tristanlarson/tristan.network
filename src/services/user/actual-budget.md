Actual Budget is a great service you can use to keep track of your finances. You can enter your transactions manually, import them from your bank, or have the service fetch the data from SimpleFIN (separate, paid service). Beware that SimpleFIN is not super reliable for unpopular financial institutions like local credit unions.
